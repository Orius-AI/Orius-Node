Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Orius Network ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Orius Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Solana Wallet Address: When you choose to enter your wallet address for token claims, we store this address to process reward distributions.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • System Information: CPU cores count, available memory (for compute contribution metrics only)
  • Approximate Location: Country/region based on IP address (for network statistics)
  • Session Data: Time spent with extension active, compute tasks completed

1.3 Optional Browsing Data (With Your Permission)

If you enable "Browsing Data Collection" in Settings, we may collect:

  • Pages visited (URLs)
  • Time spent on pages
  • Scroll depth and engagement metrics
  • Domains visited
Important: Browsing data collection is OFF by default. You must explicitly enable it. You can disable it anytime in Settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Token Distribution: To calculate and distribute $Orius token rewards to your wallet
  • Network Statistics: To display compute contribution metrics and network health
  • Network Optimization: Aggregated, anonymized patterns help improve our compute network performance (only if you opt-in)
  • Service Improvement: To enhance extension performance and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We may share data only in these cases:

  • Blockchain Transactions: Wallet addresses are visible on the public Solana blockchain when claiming tokens
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or valid legal process
  • Aggregated Data: Anonymous, aggregated statistics may be shared publicly (e.g., total network compute power)

4. Data Security

  • All data transmissions use HTTPS encryption
  • Wallet private keys are never collected or stored by us
  • Server data is protected with industry-standard security measures
  • Database access is restricted and logged

5. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Browsing Data: Processed in real-time and not stored long-term
  • Transaction History: Retained for compliance purposes

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disable Data Collection: Turn off browsing data collection anytime in Settings
  • Delete Data: Use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings
  • Access Data: Contact us to request a copy of your data
  • Uninstall: Remove the extension at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes through the extension or our website.

9. Permissions Explained

  • storage: To save your preferences and session data locally
  • alarms: To run background compute tasks at regular intervals
  • system.cpu: To display your CPU information (cores count)
  • system.memory: To display available memory
  • host permissions (optional): Only used if you enable browsing data collection
